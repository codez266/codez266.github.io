I am a fourth year PhD candidate at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. My research interests are at the intersection of Machine Learning and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI).

I develop methods for Human-AI interaction that allows AI systems to utilize information about their users and the context to improve their performance. For example, a chatbot that efficiently selects queries to pose to the user to learn about a concept, or personalize the interaction.

Projects

  • Teaching conversational agents to know their users by asking the right questions.
  • Understanding privacy risks of conversing with open domain conversational agents.
  • Modeling indoor covid-19 transmissions using agent based models.
  • Understanding decision making under Uncertainity
  • Automatically labeling low quality content on Wikipedia.
